What is VerifiedRenter.com?

Verified Renter is the internet's most efficient and trusted third party pre-rental verification service. Our company allows a Renter to fill out a universal rental application and have all of information verified by an independent third party prior to applying for a rental property. Once the Renter finds a suitable property, they will share their VR code with prospective Landlord(s) who can then instantly review the Renter’s application and verifications on our site to make an instant approval/denial decision.

What is this Certificate?

Congratulations! If you have received this certificate it means that a Verified Renter would like to apply for your rental property! This Renter has already filled out a universal rental application, uploaded the necessary supporting documentation, and has paid an application fee. The experts at VerifiedRenter.com have verified the Renter’s information and have uploaded those verifications to our website. All of this information is online and ready for your review so you can quickly and easily approve or deny the prospective Renter. Since all the work has been done for the prospective Landlords, there is NO COST TO YOU to review this prospective Renter's information on our website and to make a decision on whether or not you’d like to rent to them.

Again, there is NO COST TO LANDLORDS to review this prospective renter's application and information. We look forward to facilitating this, and all of your future, rental transactions.
